About Me

I'm a young software engineer from New Jersey, proficent in Python, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. I realized that I could put anything I wanted on the screen, and that unlocked the creativity in my brain. Since then I've taken (and still am taking) multiple Udemy courses on different languages, as well as a web development bootcamp created by Rutgers University.

Apart from coding, I also love hockey, coffee, and music (writing, playing, listening, anything you could imagine!).
